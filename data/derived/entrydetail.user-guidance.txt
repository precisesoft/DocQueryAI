  Dates: YYYY-MM-DD (today where constraints say "today’s date").
  Numbers: totalQty 3 decimals; valueGoodsAmt 2 decimals.
  Codes: ISO-2 uppercase; allow "??" fallback when unknown.

- Validation expectations:
  - Schema must pass.
  - Business rules checked by a post-validator (counts, sequence, minima, unit consistency).
